Choco Mousse

Description

This blender chocolate mousse, with espresso powder added to amp up the intensity of the chocolate flavor, is for all of you chocolate loversâ€“simple, easy, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy ice cream
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of instanpowder espresso
  • 1/2 teaspon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  1. Combine heavy cream, cocoa powder, confectioner's sugar, espresso powder, vanilla extract, and salt in a powerful blender
  2. Blend for 30 seconds
  3. Lastly, Chill at least 1 hour to let flavors meld.
